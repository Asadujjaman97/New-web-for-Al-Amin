Military Supplies

Al-Amin Traders is a DGDP enlisted supplier serving BOF, COD, Bangladesh Air Force and Bangladesh Army with military workshop and maintenance supplies, vehicle spare parts and accessories, field and camp equipment, clothing and personal equipment, and electronics and communication equipment. For ammunition and explosives support materials, see our Ordnance Supplies page.

1.2 Military Workshop & Maintenance Supplies

Armament Cleaning Kits and Bore Cleaning Chemicals

Armament cleaning kits contain the brushes, rods, patches and solvents needed to remove propellant residue, carbon fouling and corrosion from weapon bores and mechanisms. Bore cleaning chemicals include copper and carbon solvents, rust inhibitors and preservative solutions formulated to mil-spec standards. Regular cleaning with proper materials directly extends weapon service life and maintains operational accuracy.

Enquiry

Weapon Lubricants and Preservative Oils — Mil-Spec

Military-specification lubricants are formulated to reduce friction and wear across metal surfaces in firearms and ordnance mechanisms under extreme temperature and operating conditions. Preservative oils protect weapon surfaces from corrosion during storage and are applied to internal and external components after cleaning. Al-Amin Traders supplies CLP-type and LSA-type lubricants as well as long-term cosmoline-based preservatives.

Enquiry

Gunsmithing Tools and Precision Instruments

Gunsmithing tools cover the specialized hand tools required for the disassembly, repair and reassembly of small arms and crew-served weapons at military workshop level. These include armorer's benches, vise blocks, pin punches, sight adjustment tools and action wrenches manufactured to the tolerances the weapons require. Al-Amin Traders supplies complete armorer's tool kits and individual replacement tools for all standard service weapons.

Enquiry

Torque Wrenches and Calibration Equipment

Torque wrenches ensure that fasteners on weapons and ordnance systems are tightened to the precise specification required for safe and reliable function. Calibration equipment is used to verify that measuring instruments and tools maintain their accuracy within acceptable tolerances over time. Al-Amin Traders supplies both click-type and digital torque wrenches along with calibration standards traceable to international metrology certificates.

Enquiry

Barrel Inspection Equipment

Barrel inspection equipment is used to assess the internal condition of weapon barrels for erosion, wear, pitting and fouling that affect accuracy and safe service life. Borescopes, bore gauges and muzzle crown gauges allow armorers to make informed decisions on whether a barrel should be returned to service, repaired or replaced. Al-Amin Traders supplies both optical and video borescope systems along with precision bore measurement gauges.

Enquiry

Breech Mechanism Spare Parts

The breech mechanism is the assembly at the rear of a weapon or artillery piece that seals the chamber during firing and extracts the spent cartridge case. Spare parts including breech rings, obturator pads, firing pins and extractor claws are critical for maintaining continuous operational readiness. Al-Amin Traders sources these components from original or qualified equivalent manufacturers and maintains supply continuity for scheduled maintenance cycles.

Enquiry

Recoil Mechanism Repair Kits

Recoil mechanisms absorb and return the energy of firing in artillery and automatic weapons, protecting the mounting structure and enabling sustained fire. Repair kits include seals, springs, hydraulic fluid, piston rods and cylinder components needed to restore recoil systems to specification after wear or damage. Al-Amin Traders supplies complete overhaul kits with all consumable and structural components for scheduled and unscheduled maintenance.

Enquiry

1.3 Military Vehicle Spare Parts & Accessories

Engine Components for Armored Vehicles

Armored vehicle engines operate under heavy load conditions in demanding environments and require high-quality replacement components to maintain operational availability. Engine spare parts include pistons, cylinder liners, valve train components, oil pumps, fuel injectors and gasket sets matched to the specific powerplant in service. Al-Amin Traders sources components for diesel and multi-fuel engines used across armored personnel carriers, infantry fighting vehicles and support vehicles.

Enquiry

Transmission and Gearbox Parts

Military vehicle transmissions — whether manual, automatic or hydrostatic — transfer engine power to the drive system and must withstand the high torque demands of off-road and cross-country operation. Gearbox spare parts include gears, shafts, synchronizers, bearings, seals and shift forks required at depot-level maintenance intervals. Al-Amin Traders supplies these parts for a range of in-service military vehicles operated by the Bangladesh Armed Forces.

Enquiry

Track and Wheel Assembly Components

Tracked vehicles rely on a continuous loop of steel track links, drive sprockets, road wheels, return rollers and idler wheels to move across all terrain types. Track components are subject to high wear rates and require regular replacement to maintain mobility and vehicle safety. Al-Amin Traders supplies individual track links, track pins, bushings, end connectors and full replacement track sets for armored and engineer vehicles.

Enquiry

Hydraulic System Components

Hydraulic systems in military vehicles operate weapons stations, ramps, hatches, suspension systems and auxiliary equipment using pressurized fluid circuits. Components include hydraulic pumps, cylinders, control valves, accumulators, hoses and filtration units that must perform reliably across wide temperature ranges. Al-Amin Traders sources hydraulic components to military specification and can supply complete circuit overhaul kits for specific vehicle platforms.

Enquiry

Vehicle Electrical and Electronic Parts

Modern military vehicles contain extensive electrical systems covering the starter motor, alternator, lighting, intercom, navigation and weapon control electronics. Electrical spare parts include wiring harnesses, connectors, relays, fuses, control modules and junction boxes manufactured to the vibration and moisture resistance standards military vehicles require. Al-Amin Traders supplies electrical components from original equipment and qualified equivalent manufacturers.

Enquiry

Fuel System Components

Military vehicle fuel systems must deliver clean, measured fuel to the engine under all operating conditions including tilted terrain, extreme temperatures and high-demand tactical scenarios. Fuel system parts include fuel pumps, injection systems, fuel filters, lines, quick-disconnect couplings and fuel tank sealing components. Al-Amin Traders supplies fuel system components certified for use with diesel and multi-fuel military powerplants.

Enquiry

Military Vehicle Batteries — Heavy Duty

Military vehicle batteries must supply reliable starting power and support auxiliary electrical loads in vehicles that may sit dormant for extended periods or operate in extreme heat and cold. Heavy-duty military batteries are built to withstand the vibration and shock loads of off-road operation and meet the higher reserve capacity demands of vehicles with extensive electronic systems. Al-Amin Traders supplies sealed and conventional heavy-duty batteries conforming to NATO and equivalent military specifications.

Enquiry

Exhaust and Filter Systems

Exhaust systems on military vehicles direct combustion gases away from crew compartments and reduce the acoustic and thermal signature of the vehicle during operation. Filter systems covering air, fuel, oil and hydraulic fluids are among the most frequently replaced maintenance items and directly impact engine health and longevity. Al-Amin Traders supplies complete exhaust assemblies and OEM-equivalent filter sets for a wide range of in-service military vehicles.

Enquiry

1.4 Military Field & Camp Equipment

Tents and Field Shelters — Military Grade

Military-grade tents and field shelters provide temporary accommodation, command post and operational shelter for troops deployed in the field under all weather conditions. They are manufactured from heavy-duty canvas or modern synthetic materials with UV resistance, waterproofing and fire-retardant treatment to military specification. Al-Amin Traders supplies single-man, section and headquarters tent configurations along with modular shelters for medical and command applications.

Enquiry

Field Cooking Equipment and Rations Containers

Field cooking equipment allows military units to prepare hot meals in the field using portable stoves, burners, cook sets and insulated food containers. Rations containers include insulated carrying cases, bulk food containers and individual meal packs designed for rapid distribution and minimum field preparation. Al-Amin Traders supplies field kitchen equipment and container systems to COD specifications for use across Bangladesh Army field operations.

Enquiry

Military Stretchers and Casualty Equipment

Military stretchers and casualty handling equipment are essential for the rapid evacuation of wounded personnel from the point of injury to the nearest medical facility. Equipment includes folding and rigid stretchers, casualty drag handles, litter carriers and loading systems for military vehicles and helicopters. Al-Amin Traders supplies casualty handling equipment meeting military field medical standards for both army and air force medical units.

Enquiry

Field Communication Wire and Accessories

Field communication wire provides the physical cable backbone for telephone and signal networks established between command posts, observation points and unit positions in the field. Accessories include field telephones, reeling equipment, wire dispensers, connectors and splice kits that allow rapid installation and recovery of field wire networks. Al-Amin Traders supplies reels of military-grade field wire and the associated accessories for tactical signal installation teams.

Enquiry

Barbed Wire and Perimeter Security Materials

Barbed wire and concertina wire are standard perimeter obstacles used to slow, channel and deter unauthorized access to military positions, camps and installations. Perimeter security materials also include pickets, wire stakes, warning markers and detector systems used to establish defensive obstacles. Al-Amin Traders supplies standard barbed wire and high-tensile concertina coils along with the steel pickets and installation tools required for obstacle construction.

Enquiry

Sandbags and Fortification Materials

Sandbags are the primary material used to construct field fortifications including fighting positions, blast walls, bunker walls and protective revetments around ammunition and fuel storage. They are manufactured from hessian or polypropylene in standard military sizes that balance weight and filling capacity. Al-Amin Traders supplies sandbags in bulk quantities together with filling tools, tie wire and HESCO-type bastion systems for larger fortification requirements.

Enquiry

Camouflage Netting and Materials

Camouflage netting is used to conceal vehicles, equipment, artillery pieces and field installations from visual and near-infrared observation by reducing their signature against background terrain. Camouflage materials include ULCANS-type multi-spectral nets, face veil material, scrim and local vegetation attachment systems. Al-Amin Traders supplies camouflage netting systems in appropriate colour schemes for Bangladesh's operational environment along with support poles and fastening hardware.

Enquiry

1.5 Military Clothing & Personal Equipment

Military Uniforms — Various Ranks and Services

Military uniforms encompass service dress, combat dress and ceremonial dress manufactured to the specification of each branch of the Bangladesh Armed Forces. They include rank insignia, branch badges, name tapes and unit patches as authorized by the respective service headquarters. Al-Amin Traders supplies uniform items in the approved fabric, colour and construction standards for the Bangladesh Army, Air Force and associated uniformed services.

Enquiry

Combat Boots and Footwear

Military combat boots must provide ankle support, waterproofing, sole grip and durability across the full range of terrain and climate conditions encountered during operations and training. They are manufactured in full-grain leather or modern synthetic constructions with rubber or polyurethane soles, steel toe caps and moisture management liners. Al-Amin Traders supplies combat boots and military footwear to the sizing, width and performance requirements specified by defence procurement.

Enquiry

Military Belts, Pouches and Webbing

Military webbing systems carry the personal load of a soldier in the field and include the belt, yoke, ammunition pouches, utility pouches and water bottle carriers assembled into a load-bearing harness. Modern MOLLE-compatible systems allow modular configuration of pouches to suit the soldier's role and mission. Al-Amin Traders supplies both traditional and MOLLE-system webbing components in the material and colour standards specified for the Bangladesh Armed Forces.

Enquiry

Helmets and Body Armor Components

Military helmets protect the head from ballistic fragments, impact and blast effects and are manufactured from Kevlar, aramid or UHMWPE composite materials to NIJ or STANAG ballistic standards. Body armor components include hard ballistic plates, soft armor carriers, plate carriers and trauma pads configured for the threat level and mission type. Al-Amin Traders supplies helmet shells, suspension systems, retention straps and body armor components from internationally certified manufacturers.

Enquiry

Gloves, Socks and Personal Clothing Items

Personal clothing items at the individual soldier level include tactical gloves, woolen and moisture-wicking socks, thermal base layers, sweatshirts and cold-weather underlayers issued to supplement the combat uniform. Tactical gloves protect the hands during weapons handling, rope work and vehicle operations while maintaining trigger finger dexterity. Al-Amin Traders supplies these items to the material, sizing and performance specifications issued by defence procurement authorities.

Enquiry

NBC Protective Suits — Nuclear Biological Chemical

NBC protective suits provide full-body protection against chemical warfare agents, biological contaminants and radioactive particles in a contaminated environment. They consist of an outer garment with activated carbon or impermeable barrier protection, rubber gloves and overboots worn over the service uniform with a respirator completing the ensemble. Al-Amin Traders supplies NBC suits, gloves and overboots from internationally certified manufacturers, along with detector papers and decontamination materials.

Enquiry

Load-Bearing Vests and Equipment

Load-bearing vests distribute the weight of ammunition, water and equipment across the soldier's torso and replace the traditional belt-order webbing system in many modern configurations. Tactical vests include integrated magazine pouches, hydration system attachments, drag handles and radio pockets in a single wearable platform. Al-Amin Traders supplies plate carrier vests, chest rigs and tactical assault vests in configurations suited to infantry, special operations and support roles.

Enquiry

1.6 Military Electronics & Communication

Field Radios and Communication Sets

Field radios provide the primary voice and data communication link between commanders, units and headquarters in tactical operations. Modern military radios operate across VHF and UHF frequency bands with frequency-hopping, encryption and built-in COMSEC features to prevent interception. Al-Amin Traders can represent international radio manufacturers and supply communication sets through Bangladesh's formal defence procurement process including after-sales support and spare parts.

Enquiry

Signal Equipment and Accessories

Signal equipment covers the broader range of instruments and accessories used by military signal units to establish, operate and maintain communications networks in the field. This includes antenna masts and systems, signal distribution frames, patch panels, cable assemblies and power supplies for communications equipment. Al-Amin Traders supplies signal equipment accessories to support the communications infrastructure of Bangladesh Army and Air Force signal units.

Enquiry

Night Vision Device Components

Night vision devices allow soldiers and vehicle crews to observe and operate in darkness by amplifying available ambient light through image intensifier tubes or detecting thermal infrared signatures. Components include image intensifier tubes, objective lenses, eyepiece assemblies, mounting brackets and battery compartments required for servicing in-service night vision equipment. Al-Amin Traders sources night vision components and complete devices from qualified international manufacturers for supply through DGDP channels.

Enquiry

Optical Sighting Systems and Accessories

Optical sighting systems include telescopic sights, reflex sights, laser rangefinders and spotting scopes used on individual weapons, crew-served weapons and armored vehicles to improve target acquisition and engagement accuracy. Accessories include mounts, rings, lens covers, reticle illuminators and boresighting tools. Al-Amin Traders supplies optical sighting systems and the accessories required for their installation and maintenance on service weapons.

Enquiry

Surveillance Equipment

Military surveillance equipment covers ground surveillance radars, thermal imaging cameras, unattended ground sensors and perimeter monitoring systems used to detect and track movement around installations and field positions. These systems provide persistent situational awareness beyond the range of visual observation and in low-visibility conditions. Al-Amin Traders can represent international surveillance system manufacturers and supply their equipment through Bangladesh's defence procurement framework.

Enquiry

Electronic Warfare Support Components

Electronic warfare support components include the hardware and subsystems used in signal intelligence, jamming and electronic protection equipment operated by specialist military units. These encompass antenna elements, frequency scanning receivers, signal processors and power amplifier modules used in electronic warfare platforms. Al-Amin Traders acts as the local procurement channel for international EW component suppliers seeking to supply Bangladesh's defence electronics requirements through regulated channels.

Enquiry

Military Cables and Connectors

Military-grade cables and connectors are manufactured to withstand the vibration, temperature extremes, moisture and abrasion encountered in defence applications where commercial-grade wiring would fail. These include MIL-SPEC shielded cable assemblies, circular connectors, RF coaxial cables and fiber optic assemblies used throughout military vehicle, aircraft and communication system installations. Al-Amin Traders supplies MIL-SPEC cable and connector assemblies for maintenance and new installation requirements across all three services.
