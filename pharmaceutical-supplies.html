Pharmaceutical Supplies (EDCL)

Al-Amin Traders supplies the Essential Drugs Company Limited (EDCL) with active pharmaceutical ingredients, pharmaceutical excipients, packaging materials, laboratory equipment, manufacturing consumables, and medical devices and hospital supplies.

Back to Home Page

3.1 Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs)

Antibiotics Raw Materials — Amoxicillin, Ampicillin, Ciprofloxacin

Antibiotic APIs are the active chemical substances from which finished antibiotic medicines are manufactured by pharmaceutical companies. Common APIs supplied include Amoxicillin trihydrate, Ampicillin anhydrous and Ciprofloxacin hydrochloride used in the production of capsules, tablets and injectable antibiotics. Al-Amin Traders sources these APIs from internationally qualified manufacturers holding WHO-GMP and relevant pharmacopoeia certifications for supply to EDCL's production requirements.

Enquiry

Analgesic and Antipyretic APIs — Paracetamol, Ibuprofen, Aspirin

Analgesic and antipyretic APIs form the basis of the most widely consumed medicines in Bangladesh's essential drug program including paracetamol tablets, ibuprofen capsules and aspirin. These APIs must meet BP, USP or IP pharmacopoeia specifications for purity, particle size and microbial limits before acceptance at EDCL's manufacturing facility. Al-Amin Traders supplies these high-volume APIs in bulk quantities with full certificate of analysis documentation and regulatory compliance support.

Enquiry

Antihypertensive APIs

Antihypertensive APIs are the active ingredients used in the manufacture of medicines for the treatment of high blood pressure, one of the most prevalent chronic conditions in Bangladesh's population. These include Amlodipine besylate, Atenolol, Enalapril maleate and Losartan potassium supplied to the quality specifications of major international pharmacopoeias. Al-Amin Traders supplies antihypertensive APIs from regulated manufacturing sources with full documentation packages for EDCL's procurement and quality assurance review.

Enquiry

Antidiabetic APIs — Metformin, Glibenclamide

Antidiabetic APIs are used in the production of medicines for the management of Type 2 diabetes, a condition affecting a large and growing proportion of Bangladesh's population. Metformin hydrochloride and Glibenclamide are the two core antidiabetic APIs in Bangladesh's essential medicines list and are required in significant volumes for EDCL's tablet manufacturing program. Al-Amin Traders supplies both APIs in the particle size grades and purity specifications required for direct compression or wet granulation manufacturing processes.

Enquiry

Antiparasitic and Antimalarial APIs

Antiparasitic APIs are used in medicines targeting helminth infections, protozoal diseases and malaria that remain significant public health concerns in parts of Bangladesh. APIs in this category include Albendazole, Mebendazole, Chloroquine phosphate and Artemisinin derivatives used in WHO-recommended treatment regimens. Al-Amin Traders sources these APIs from manufacturers holding WHO prequalification where applicable for supply to EDCL's essential medicine production program.

Enquiry

Vitamins and Nutritional Supplement Raw Materials

Vitamin APIs and nutritional supplement raw materials are used in the manufacture of multivitamin tablets, iron supplements and therapeutic nutritional products distributed through Bangladesh's public health programs. APIs include Vitamin C, Vitamin B complex fractions, Folic acid, Ferrous sulfate and Zinc sulfate. Al-Amin Traders supplies these raw materials to the food and pharmaceutical grade specifications required for finished product manufacture at EDCL.

Enquiry

Cardiovascular Drug APIs

Cardiovascular APIs are used in the manufacture of medicines for heart failure, arrhythmia and lipid control that form part of Bangladesh's essential medicines program. This category includes Digoxin, Simvastatin, Warfarin sodium, Isosorbide dinitrate and other cardiovascular active substances. Al-Amin Traders supplies cardiovascular APIs from internationally licensed pharmaceutical chemical manufacturers with the complete documentation and cold chain management required for these sensitive materials.

Enquiry

Respiratory Drug APIs

Respiratory APIs are used in the production of bronchodilators, corticosteroid inhalers and oral respiratory medicines for asthma, COPD and respiratory infection treatment. APIs in this category include Salbutamol sulfate, Theophylline, Beclomethasone dipropionate and Prednisolone. Al-Amin Traders supplies respiratory APIs in the inhalation, oral and injectable grades required by EDCL's manufacturing specifications, with full analytical testing documentation.

3.2 Pharmaceutical Excipients

Microcrystalline Cellulose — MCC

Microcrystalline cellulose is the most widely used tablet excipient in the pharmaceutical industry, functioning as a filler-binder in both direct compression and wet granulation tablet manufacturing processes. It is available in multiple particle size grades and is characterized by its compressibility, flow properties and chemical inertness. Al-Amin Traders supplies MCC in the grades specified in EDCL's product formulations including the high-density grades used in direct compression applications.

Enquiry

Lactose Monohydrate

Lactose monohydrate is a pharmaceutical-grade sugar widely used as a tablet filler-diluent and as the carrier in dry powder inhalation products. It provides good compressibility, acceptable taste and compatibility with most active ingredients at the purity levels required by BP and USP pharmacopoeias. Al-Amin Traders supplies lactose monohydrate in sieved and milled grades for EDCL's oral solid dosage form manufacturing requirements.

Enquiry

Starch — Corn, Potato and Pregelatinized

Pharmaceutical-grade starch is used as a tablet binder, disintegrant and diluent in oral solid dosage form manufacturing. Corn and potato starch are used in wet granulation processes while pregelatinized starch offers better flow and direct compressibility for modern tablet manufacturing. Al-Amin Traders supplies pharmaceutical-grade starch in all three forms to the pharmacopoeial specifications required by EDCL's formulation requirements.

Enquiry

Talc and Magnesium Stearate

Talc is used as a glidant and lubricant in tablet manufacture and as a dusting agent in powder formulations to improve powder flow and reduce sticking. Magnesium stearate is the most widely used tablet lubricant, preventing adhesion of the tablet mass to punch faces and dies during compression. Al-Amin Traders supplies pharmaceutical-grade talc and magnesium stearate meeting BP/USP specifications in the particle size grades and purity levels required by EDCL's quality standards.

Enquiry

Povidone — PVP Grades

Povidone is a synthetic polymer used as a tablet binder in wet granulation manufacturing and as a film-forming agent in tablet coatings and eye drop preparations. It is available in multiple K-value grades with K30 being the most commonly used binder grade and K90 used in specialized controlled-release applications. Al-Amin Traders supplies pharmaceutical-grade PVP in the K-value grades specified in EDCL's approved product formulations.

Enquiry

Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose — HPMC

HPMC is a cellulose derivative with multiple pharmaceutical applications including tablet coating, controlled-release matrix formulation, viscosity modification in liquid preparations and binder function in wet granulation. It is available in viscosity grades from low-viscosity coating grades to high-viscosity controlled-release matrix grades. Al-Amin Traders supplies HPMC in the viscosity and substitution grades specified for each of EDCL's product formulations requiring this excipient.

Enquiry

Croscarmellose Sodium and Disintegrants

Disintegrants are excipients that promote the rapid breakup of a compressed tablet when it contacts water or gastrointestinal fluid, releasing the active ingredient for absorption. Croscarmellose sodium is a superdisintegrant used in low concentrations to achieve rapid disintegration times in both conventional and dispersible tablet formulations. Al-Amin Traders supplies croscarmellose sodium, sodium starch glycolate and crospovidone in the pharmacopoeial grades specified by EDCL's formulation team.

Enquiry

Colorants and Coating Polymers — Eudragit and Opadry

Pharmaceutical colorants are approved dyes and lakes used to identify different tablet strengths and improve product appearance in line with regulatory guidelines. Coating polymers including Eudragit acrylic polymers and Opadry ready-to-use coating systems provide the film coat that protects tablets from moisture, masks taste and enables modified release profiles. Al-Amin Traders supplies these specialty coating materials from their original manufacturers for EDCL's tablet coating operations.

Enquiry

Flavoring Agents and Sweeteners

Pharmaceutical flavoring agents and sweeteners are added to oral liquid preparations, chewable tablets and dispersible formulations to improve palatability and patient compliance, particularly in pediatric products. Permitted sweeteners include sucrose, sorbitol, mannitol, aspartame and saccharin while flavoring agents are approved synthetic or natural flavor concentrates. Al-Amin Traders supplies pharmaceutical-grade sweeteners and flavoring concentrates in the quality and purity grades required for oral pharmaceutical preparation.

Enquiry

3.3 Pharmaceutical Packaging Materials

HDPE Bottles and Containers — Pharmaceutical Grade

Pharmaceutical-grade HDPE bottles provide moisture barrier protection and chemical compatibility for tablet, capsule and liquid medicine products distributed through Bangladesh's public health system. They are manufactured from virgin resin, tested for migration and permeation limits and supplied with child-resistant or standard closure systems as specified. Al-Amin Traders supplies HDPE bottles and containers in the standard and custom sizes required by EDCL's tablet and liquid product lines.

Enquiry

Blister Foil — Aluminum and PVC/PVDC

Blister packaging seals individual tablets or capsules in a formed plastic cavity sealed with a push-through aluminum lidding foil, providing unit-dose protection against moisture, oxygen and contamination. PVC and PVDC base films are thermoformed to the blister pocket shape while aluminum foil with heat-seal lacquer forms the lidding material. Al-Amin Traders supplies blister base films and aluminum lidding foil in the widths and gauges compatible with EDCL's blister packaging machines.

Enquiry

Pharmaceutical Cartons and Folding Boxes

Pharmaceutical folding cartons are the outer secondary packaging that contains the primary pack and carries the product labeling, dosage instructions and regulatory information. They are manufactured from SBS or recycled board with printed text and barcodes to EDCL's approved artwork and are designed to lock securely around the blister or bottle. Al-Amin Traders supplies printed pharmaceutical cartons and plain carton board in the sizes specified for each of EDCL's packaged product lines.

Enquiry

Package Inserts and Labels

Package inserts are the printed information leaflets included with pharmaceutical products that provide full prescribing information, patient instructions, contraindications and storage conditions. Pharmaceutical labels are printed on pressure-sensitive stock or heat-shrink material and carry the required regulatory information for each product. Al-Amin Traders supplies printed package inserts and labels produced to EDCL's approved artwork with the print quality and paper grades required by pharmaceutical packaging standards.

Enquiry

Desiccants and Moisture Absorbers

Pharmaceutical desiccants are placed inside tablet and capsule containers to absorb ambient moisture and protect moisture-sensitive drug products from degradation during their shelf life in Bangladesh's humid tropical climate. Silica gel, molecular sieve and clay desiccants are available in sachet and canister formats sized to the container volume and the product's moisture sensitivity requirements. Al-Amin Traders supplies pharmaceutical-grade desiccants with certificates of conformity in the standard sizes used in EDCL's packaging lines.

Enquiry

Tamper-Evident Closures and Caps

Tamper-evident closures provide a visible indication of whether a medicine container has been opened before the consumer receives it, protecting against tampering, adulteration and counterfeiting. They include induction-sealed caps, shrink bands, ratchet ring closures and child-resistant push-and-turn caps manufactured to pharmaceutical industry standards. Al-Amin Traders supplies tamper-evident caps and closure systems in the neck finishes compatible with EDCL's HDPE and glass container range.

Enquiry

Ampoules and Vials — Glass and Plastic

Glass ampoules and vials are the primary containers for injectable pharmaceutical products and must be manufactured from Type I borosilicate glass that meets pharmacopoeial hydrolytic resistance requirements. Plastic vials in polypropylene and COP/COC materials are used for products where glass is unsuitable. Al-Amin Traders supplies ampoules in standard 1ml to 20ml sizes and vials in crimp-top and screw-cap configurations for EDCL's injectable product manufacturing requirements.

Enquiry

IV Bags and Infusion Containers

Intravenous infusion bags and containers are used for the large-volume parenteral products including normal saline, dextrose and Ringer's solution manufactured and distributed by EDCL to government hospitals. IV bags are manufactured from PVC or non-PVC polyolefin films with integrated infusion ports and additive injection sites. Al-Amin Traders supplies IV bag films, port assemblies and complete bag sets in the 250ml, 500ml and 1000ml sizes used in EDCL's infusion manufacturing line.

Enquiry

3.4 Pharmaceutical Laboratory Equipment

Analytical Balances and Precision Weighing Equipment

Analytical balances are used throughout pharmaceutical quality control and manufacturing for the precise weighing of API quantities, excipient charges, sample preparations and reference standard solutions. They must meet the accuracy requirements of pharmacopoeial methods and be capable of calibration verification against certified reference weights. Al-Amin Traders supplies analytical balances, precision balances and moisture analyzers from internationally recognized laboratory instrument manufacturers for EDCL's quality control and manufacturing weighing requirements.

Enquiry

HPLC Systems and Accessories

High-performance liquid chromatography is the primary analytical technique used in pharmaceutical quality control for the identification, assay and impurity profiling of drug substances and finished dosage forms. HPLC systems consist of a solvent delivery pump, autosampler, column oven, detector and data system. Al-Amin Traders supplies complete HPLC systems and accessories including columns, detector lamps, inlet filters and spare pump seals from internationally recognized analytical instrument manufacturers for EDCL's quality control laboratory.

Enquiry

Dissolution Testing Apparatus

Dissolution testing is a mandatory quality control test for oral solid dosage forms that measures the rate and extent to which the active ingredient is released from the tablet or capsule under standardized conditions. Dissolution apparatus consists of a temperature-controlled water bath, rotating paddle or basket assemblies and sample collection ports. Al-Amin Traders supplies USP Apparatus 1 and 2 dissolution testers with automatic sampling accessories for EDCL's finished product quality release testing program.

Enquiry

Tablet Hardness Testers

Tablet hardness testers measure the crushing strength of compressed tablets to verify that the compression force used during manufacturing produces tablets with sufficient mechanical integrity for handling, packaging and transportation. Both manual and automated hardness testers are used in in-process quality control during tablet compression. Al-Amin Traders supplies manual and motorized tablet hardness testers calibrated to display results in Newtons and kiloponds for EDCL's tablet production quality control.

Enquiry

Disintegration Testing Equipment

Disintegration testing measures the time taken for a tablet or capsule to break down in a simulated gastrointestinal fluid medium under standardized agitation conditions as specified in BP and USP monographs. Disintegration testers consist of a tube rack assembly that moves tablets up and down through a fluid bath at a controlled temperature. Al-Amin Traders supplies USP-compliant disintegration testers and the basket assemblies, discs and fluid heating systems required for routine pharmacopoeial testing at EDCL.

Enquiry

Friability Testers

Friability testing determines the tendency of uncoated tablets to chip, abrade or break when subjected to mechanical handling and is a standard in-process test performed during tablet compression. The friability tester rotates tablets in a drum at a set speed for a defined number of rotations after which the percentage weight loss is calculated. Al-Amin Traders supplies friability testers meeting USP and BP apparatus specifications for EDCL's tablet manufacturing quality control program.

Enquiry

pH Meters and Conductivity Meters

pH meters are used throughout pharmaceutical manufacturing and quality control for the measurement of solution pH in API dissolution testing, buffer preparation, water quality monitoring and product formulation. Conductivity meters measure the ionic content of purified water and water for injection to verify compliance with pharmacopoeial water quality specifications. Al-Amin Traders supplies bench-top and portable pH and conductivity meters with calibration buffer solutions and electrode accessories for EDCL's water system and laboratory use.

Enquiry

Spectrophotometers — UV/Vis

UV/Vis spectrophotometry is used in pharmaceutical quality control for the identification and quantitative assay of drug substances that absorb ultraviolet or visible light, as specified in numerous pharmacopoeial monographs. The technique is also used for colour measurement, water quality assessment and raw material identification. Al-Amin Traders supplies single-beam and double-beam UV/Vis spectrophotometers with UV analysis software and reference cuvette sets for EDCL's quality control laboratory.

Enquiry

Microscopes — Optical and Digital

Optical and digital microscopes are used in pharmaceutical quality control for the visual identification of raw materials, examination of particle morphology, detection of contamination and microbial examination of non-sterile products. Digital microscopes with camera systems allow image capture and measurement for documentation and comparative analysis. Al-Amin Traders supplies laboratory microscopes in compound and stereo configurations with digital camera interfaces and calibrated eyepiece micrometers for EDCL's quality control applications.

Enquiry

3.5 Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Consumables

Filter Membranes and Cartridges

Pharmaceutical filter membranes and cartridges are used for the sterile filtration of injectable preparations, the clarification of liquid products and the air filtration of cleanroom HVAC and process air systems. Membrane materials including PVDF, nylon and PTFE are selected based on compatibility with the product being filtered. Al-Amin Traders supplies sterile filtration membranes, filter cartridges and capsule filters in the pore sizes and membrane materials required by EDCL's sterile and non-sterile manufacturing operations.

Enquiry

Sterile Gloves and Cleanroom Garments

Pharmaceutical cleanroom garments prevent the contamination of sterile and non-sterile pharmaceutical products by skin particles, hair and microorganisms shed from personnel working in the manufacturing environment. Sterile gloves, coveralls, head covers, shoe covers and face masks are worn in graded cleanroom areas according to the contamination risk of the activity. Al-Amin Traders supplies cleanroom garments and sterile gloves in the sizes and classifications required for EDCL's pharmaceutical manufacturing grade areas.

Enquiry

Laboratory Chemicals and Reagents — Analytical Grade

Analytical grade reagents are the chemical solvents, acids, bases and reference solutions used in pharmaceutical quality control testing to prepare mobile phases, standard solutions and test media as specified in pharmacopoeial methods. These must meet defined purity specifications to ensure testing results are accurate and reliable. Al-Amin Traders supplies HPLC-grade solvents, ACS-grade reagents and volumetric solutions from internationally recognized chemical manufacturers for EDCL's quality control laboratory.

Enquiry

Reference Standards and Calibration Materials

Certified reference standards are highly characterized chemical substances used in pharmaceutical testing as comparators against which the identity and potency of drug substances and products are measured. They are supplied by pharmacopoeial authorities including BP, USP and WHO and must be stored and handled under strict conditions to maintain their integrity. Al-Amin Traders supplies pharmacopoeial reference standards and in-house working standard materials to EDCL's quality control laboratory.

Enquiry

Cleaning Agents and Sanitizers — Pharmaceutical Grade

Pharmaceutical manufacturing cleaning agents and sanitizers are used to maintain the microbiological cleanliness of production equipment, manufacturing rooms and cleanroom surfaces to the levels required by GMP guidelines. They include detergent cleaning agents, alcohol-based surface sanitizers, sporicidal agents and purified water for equipment rinsing. Al-Amin Traders supplies validated pharmaceutical cleaning and sanitization chemicals with the compatibility and efficacy data required to support EDCL's cleaning validation program.

Enquiry

Purified Water System Components

Purified water is the most widely used material in pharmaceutical manufacturing and the purification system's components must be maintained to consistently produce water meeting pharmacopoeial specifications. Components include reverse osmosis membranes, mixed bed ion exchange resins, UV sterilizer lamps, conductivity sensors, pre-filter cartridges and distribution loop sanitation accessories. Al-Amin Traders supplies purified water system consumables and replacement components for the water treatment infrastructure at EDCL's manufacturing facility.

Enquiry

Sterile Containers and Transfer Equipment

Sterile containers and transfer equipment are used to transport pharmaceutical bulk products, sterile solutions and granules within and between production areas while maintaining product sterility and preventing cross-contamination. Equipment includes sterile product containers, closed transfer systems, dip tubes and sanitary fittings used in injectable and sterile product manufacturing. Al-Amin Traders supplies sterile stainless steel and single-use container systems and transfer equipment for EDCL's sterile manufacturing and filling operations.

Enquiry

3.6 Medical Devices & Hospital Supplies

Surgical Gloves — Latex and Nitrile

Surgical gloves provide a sterile barrier between the surgeon's hands and the patient during operative procedures and must meet ISO 10282 standards for sterility, dimensions, tensile properties and freedom from holes. Latex gloves offer the tactile sensitivity preferred for fine surgical work while nitrile gloves are used where latex allergy is a concern. Al-Amin Traders supplies sterile surgical gloves in both latex and nitrile materials in the half-size range required for surgical use in Bangladesh's government hospital network.

Enquiry

Surgical Masks and Protective Equipment

Surgical masks protect the patient from respiratory secretions of the operating team and are a standard infection control measure in all operating theatres and clinical areas. Personal protective equipment for healthcare workers includes gowns, face shields, shoe covers and head covers required in clinical and surgical environments. Al-Amin Traders supplies surgical masks to ASTM F2100 or equivalent standards and full PPE sets for distribution through EDCL's medical supply program.

Enquiry

Syringes and Needles

Disposable syringes and needles are among the highest-volume medical devices used in Bangladesh's healthcare system for the administration of injectable medicines, vaccines and fluids in hospital and community health settings. Auto-disable syringes are the WHO-recommended standard for immunization programs. Al-Amin Traders supplies standard luer-lock and luer-slip syringes from 1ml to 50ml and injection needles in the gauge and length range required by EDCL's medical device supply program.

Enquiry

IV Cannulas and Catheters

Intravenous cannulas are inserted into peripheral veins to provide vascular access for fluid therapy, blood transfusion and intravenous medication administration in hospital patients. Urinary catheters are used for bladder drainage in surgical and medical patients and are supplied in Foley and straight catheter configurations. Al-Amin Traders supplies IV cannulas in the gauge range 14G to 26G and Foley catheters in silicone and latex in the French sizes used in Bangladesh's government hospital system.

Enquiry

Wound Dressing and Bandages

Wound dressings and bandages are fundamental consumable medical supplies required in every level of Bangladesh's healthcare system from community health centers to tertiary hospitals. Products include sterile gauze swabs, adhesive wound dressings, crepe bandages, elastic adhesive bandages and non-adherent wound contact layers. Al-Amin Traders supplies wound care products to the specifications and packaging requirements of EDCL's distribution program for government health facilities.

Enquiry

Surgical Sutures

Surgical sutures are used to close surgical incisions and traumatic wounds and are available in absorbable and non-absorbable materials in a wide range of gauges and needle configurations. Absorbable sutures including polyglycolic acid and chromic gut are used for internal tissue closure while non-absorbable materials like polypropylene and nylon are used for skin closure. Al-Amin Traders supplies both absorbable and non-absorbable sutures in the range of sizes and needle types required by EDCL's hospital supply program.

Enquiry

Diagnostic Test Kits

Rapid diagnostic test kits allow point-of-care testing for malaria, dengue, typhoid, hepatitis, HIV and other conditions without the need for laboratory infrastructure, making them particularly valuable in Bangladesh's rural and district health facilities. Test kits provide a result within minutes using a lateral flow immunoassay format. Al-Amin Traders supplies WHO-prequalified diagnostic test kits from internationally certified manufacturers for inclusion in EDCL's essential medical device supply program.
