Naval Supplies (NSSD)

Al-Amin Traders supports the Naval Store Sub Depot (NSSD) and Bangladesh Navy with marine engineering components, deck equipment and hardware, marine electrical and electronics, marine coatings and chemicals, ship safety and survival equipment, naval workshop supplies, and vessel maintenance materials.

2.1 Marine Engineering Components

Marine Diesel Engine Spare Parts

Naval vessels rely on marine diesel engines for main propulsion and auxiliary power and require a constant supply of spare parts to maintain operational availability through scheduled and unscheduled maintenance. Engine spare parts include pistons, liner kits, fuel injection equipment, turbocharger assemblies, cooling system parts and complete overhaul gasket sets matched to the specific engine model in service. Al-Amin Traders sources genuine and OEM-equivalent spare parts for the diesel engine types operated by the Bangladesh Navy.

Enquiry

Marine Gear and Propulsion Components

Marine gearboxes and reduction gears transmit engine power to the propeller shaft at the correct rotational speed and direction for efficient propulsion. Propulsion components include gearbox internals, clutch packs, output flanges, coupling elements and thrust bearings that must withstand the continuous load of open-sea operation. Al-Amin Traders supplies gearbox spare parts and propulsion drive components for naval vessels maintained through the NSSD.

Enquiry

Stern Tube and Propeller Shaft Materials

The stern tube is the watertight housing through which the propeller shaft passes from the engine room through the hull and into the water. Stern tube components include cutlass bearings, lip seals, seal housing assemblies and lubrication systems that prevent water ingress and support the rotating shaft. Al-Amin Traders supplies stern tube and shaft sealing components along with bronze propeller shaft materials for routine and drydock maintenance cycles.

Enquiry

Marine Pumps and Pump Components

Marine pumps are used throughout a naval vessel for sea water cooling, fresh water distribution, bilge drainage, fuel transfer, firefighting and ballast management. Pump components include impellers, wear rings, mechanical seals, shaft sleeves and bearing assemblies that degrade over time and require regular replacement. Al-Amin Traders supplies centrifugal, gear and diaphragm pump assemblies and all associated wearing parts for the pump types in service with the Bangladesh Navy.

Enquiry

Marine Compressors and Accessories

Marine compressors supply compressed air for engine starting, torpedo systems, diving operations, control systems and general shipboard pneumatic tools and equipment. Accessories include air receivers, pressure relief valves, moisture separators, after-coolers and compressor valve assemblies used in maintenance and overhaul. Al-Amin Traders supplies high-pressure and low-pressure marine compressor assemblies and the consumable parts required for scheduled overhaul intervals.

Enquiry

Heat Exchangers and Cooling System Parts

Marine heat exchangers transfer heat from engine jacket water, lubricating oil and charge air to sea water cooling circuits, maintaining engine temperatures within safe operating limits. Cooling system parts include heat exchanger bundles, end covers, sacrificial anodes, expansion tanks, thermostats and coolant hoses. Al-Amin Traders supplies heat exchanger units and cooling system components for the diesel engine and auxiliary machinery plants of Bangladesh Navy vessels.

Enquiry

Marine Gaskets, Seals and O-Rings

Marine gaskets and seals are critical consumable items that prevent leakage of oil, water, fuel and gas at every joint and connection point throughout the machinery plant of a naval vessel. O-rings, flange gaskets, cylinder head gaskets, valve stem seals and stuffing box packing are replaced at every maintenance interval to maintain system integrity. Al-Amin Traders supplies comprehensive gasket and seal kits in the materials and dimensions required for all engine and mechanical system overhauls.

2.2 Naval Deck Equipment & Hardware

Marine Ropes, Wire Ropes and Mooring Lines

Mooring lines, towing hawsers and working ropes are essential deck equipment used for berthing vessels, towing, lifting and securing operations at sea and in harbour. Marine ropes are manufactured from polyester, polypropylene, nylon and mixed fiber constructions while wire ropes provide the strength required for winch and crane applications. Al-Amin Traders supplies mooring lines, wire ropes and deck rope requirements in the diameters and breaking strengths specified for each vessel class.

Enquiry

Anchor Chains and Shackles

Anchor chains connect the vessel's anchor to the windlass and must be strong enough to hold the ship in position under storm conditions and current loads. Shackles are the connecting links used throughout shipboard rigging and lifting applications and are manufactured in bow, dee and safety locking patterns to Lloyd's or equivalent classification society standards. Al-Amin Traders supplies anchor chain in studlink and stud-less configurations and marine shackles in the proof load ratings required by naval specification.

Enquiry

Deck Fittings and Cleats

Deck fittings include the hardware mounted to the vessel's deck and hull for securing mooring lines, towing connections and deck equipment. Cleats, bitts, fairleads, bollards and chocks are cast or fabricated in marine-grade steel or bronze and are through-bolted to structural deck members to withstand the full load of mooring and towing forces. Al-Amin Traders supplies deck hardware fittings in the sizes and material grades specified for naval vessels operating in tropical marine environments.

Enquiry

Marine Pulleys, Blocks and Rigging Hardware

Marine blocks and pulleys are used in cargo handling, boat launching, rigging and deck machinery applications to redirect and multiply rope or wire rope tensions. Rigging hardware includes turnbuckles, snap hooks, eye bolts, thimbles and swivels used to assemble and secure shipboard rope and wire systems. Al-Amin Traders supplies marine blocks, rigging hardware and associated components in stainless steel, galvanized steel and bronze for the Bangladesh Navy's vessel maintenance requirements.

Enquiry

Winch Components and Parts

Deck winches are used for anchor handling, mooring, towing and crane operations and are driven by electric, hydraulic or manual power depending on the vessel's design. Winch spare parts include drum assemblies, brake bands, worm gears, motor couplings, wire rope guides and control valve assemblies required at overhaul intervals. Al-Amin Traders supplies winch components and complete winch assemblies for the vessel types operated by the Bangladesh Navy.

Enquiry

Marine Hoses and Couplings

Marine hoses carry fuel, water, hydraulic fluid and seawater cooling in flexible runs throughout the vessel's machinery spaces, deck and hull penetrations. Couplings include camlock fittings, flanged connections, quick-release couplers and fuelling hose assemblies used for replenishment and transfer operations. Al-Amin Traders supplies marine hose assemblies in the bore sizes, pressure ratings and material grades required for naval vessel machinery and deck operations.

Enquiry

Navigation Lights and Fixtures

Navigation lights are mandatory safety equipment required by COLREGS that indicate a vessel's position, heading and status to other ships at sea and in harbour. Naval vessel navigation lights are manufactured in sealed, waterproof and shock-resistant housings rated for continuous operation in the marine environment. Al-Amin Traders supplies LED and incandescent navigation light assemblies, replacement lenses and wiring harnesses for all standard navigation light positions on naval vessels.

Enquiry

2.3 Marine Electrical & Electronics

Marine Electrical Cables and Wiring

Marine electrical cables are manufactured with tinned copper conductors, oil-resistant insulation and flame-retardant outer sheaths to meet the fire safety and corrosion requirements of a shipboard environment. They carry power distribution, control signals and data throughout the vessel's electrical system and must be replaced when damaged or when systems are upgraded. Al-Amin Traders supplies marine cable in the standard conductor sizes, voltage ratings and sheath types specified for naval vessel electrical systems.

Enquiry

Marine Circuit Breakers and Switchgear

Marine circuit breakers and switchgear protect the vessel's electrical distribution system by interrupting fault currents and allowing safe isolation of individual circuits for maintenance. Switchgear panels, molded case circuit breakers and miniature circuit breakers used in naval vessels must be rated for the vibration, humidity and salt air conditions of the marine environment. Al-Amin Traders supplies marine-rated circuit breakers and switchgear assemblies for the power distribution systems of Bangladesh Navy vessels.

Enquiry

Navigation Instruments and Spare Parts

Naval vessel navigation instruments include magnetic compasses, gyrocompasses, GPS receivers, depth sounders and speed logs that provide the data needed for safe navigation at sea. Spare parts cover replacement sensors, transducers, display units and interface electronics needed to maintain these systems in continuous operation. Al-Amin Traders supplies navigation instrument spares and complete replacement units from internationally recognized marine electronics manufacturers.

Enquiry

Radar System Components and Accessories

Marine radar provides surface and air surveillance around a vessel and is an essential navigation and collision avoidance tool on all naval craft. Radar system components include magnetrons, motor assemblies, scanners, display units, waveguide sections and power supplies that degrade with operational hours. Al-Amin Traders supplies radar components and accessories for the radar systems installed on Bangladesh Navy vessels and can support installation of upgraded radar systems.

Enquiry

Sonar Equipment Accessories

Sonar systems are used by naval vessels for submarine detection, mine avoidance and underwater mapping using acoustic energy transmitted through the water. Sonar accessories include transducer elements, hydrophones, dome windows, signal processing boards and cabling required for system maintenance and repair. Al-Amin Traders acts as the local procurement channel for sonar equipment accessories through Bangladesh's naval procurement framework.

Enquiry

Marine Batteries and Charging Systems

Marine batteries provide emergency power for lighting, communication and safety equipment when the vessel's main generators are unavailable. They are also used as the primary power source for submarines and small craft and as the starting battery bank for main and auxiliary engines. Al-Amin Traders supplies sealed lead-acid, AGM and lithium-based marine batteries along with the battery chargers and monitoring systems required for naval vessel battery installations.

Enquiry

Waterproof Connectors and Junction Boxes

Waterproof electrical connectors and junction boxes prevent moisture, salt spray and condensation from causing short circuits and corrosion failures in the electrical systems of naval vessels. They are rated to IP66 or higher ingress protection standards and are manufactured from marine-grade materials compatible with the shipboard environment. Al-Amin Traders supplies waterproof connector assemblies and junction boxes in the sizes and IP ratings required for deck, hull and engine room electrical installations.

2.4 Marine Coatings & Chemicals

Anti-Corrosion Coatings for Hulls

Hull anti-corrosion coatings form the primary barrier between the steel structure of a naval vessel and the corrosive seawater environment. They consist of an epoxy primer system applied to blast-cleaned steel, building up to a total dry film thickness specified by the paint manufacturer and vessel classification requirements. Al-Amin Traders supplies full hull coating systems from international marine paint manufacturers including surface preparation materials and application consumables.

Enquiry

Anti-Fouling Paints and Coatings

Anti-fouling paint is applied to the underwater hull of naval vessels to prevent the attachment and growth of marine organisms including barnacles, algae and tube worms that increase drag and fuel consumption. Modern anti-fouling systems use controlled-release biocide or foul-release silicone technology to maintain a clean hull surface between drydock periods. Al-Amin Traders supplies anti-fouling paint systems in self-polishing and hard coating formulations appropriate for the operational profile of Bangladesh Navy vessels.

Enquiry

Marine Lubricants and Greases

Marine lubricants are formulated for the specific demands of shipboard machinery including high-load diesel engines, gearboxes, stern tubes and deck machinery operating in a salt-laden environment. Marine greases are used in stern tube bearings, wire rope lubrication, deck machinery and anchor chain lubrication where oil-based lubricants cannot be retained. Al-Amin Traders supplies marine engine oils, gear oils and specialty greases meeting the specifications of the machinery manufacturers in service with the Bangladesh Navy.

Enquiry

Bilge Cleaning Chemicals

Bilge cleaning chemicals are used to break down and emulsify the mixture of oil, fuel, grease and seawater that accumulates in the bilges of naval vessels during normal machinery operation. Effective bilge cleaning reduces the risk of bilge pump blockage, fire hazard and marine pollution during bilge discharge operations in port. Al-Amin Traders supplies biodegradable bilge cleaners, degreasers and bilge treatment chemicals that meet MARPOL compliance requirements.

Enquiry

Rust Converters and Inhibitors

Rust converters chemically react with iron oxide corrosion on steel surfaces to convert it into a stable compound that can be overcoated without the need for complete blast cleaning. Rust inhibitors are applied to cleaned metal surfaces as a temporary protection during drydock periods before the permanent coating system is applied. Al-Amin Traders supplies rust converter and inhibitor products as part of the complete hull maintenance chemical package for naval vessel drydock requirements.

Enquiry

Marine Sealants and Adhesives

Marine sealants are used to waterproof deck fittings, port lights, hatches and hull penetrations to prevent water ingress and corrosion at joint interfaces. Marine adhesives bond dissimilar materials, secure deck fittings and repair composite and GRP structures with bonds that resist the constant flexing, vibration and chemical exposure of the marine environment. Al-Amin Traders supplies polysulfide, polyurethane and silicone marine sealants along with structural marine adhesives for shipboard maintenance applications.

Enquiry

Engine Room Cleaning Chemicals

Engine room cleaning chemicals are specially formulated to remove carbon deposits, oil contamination, scale and salt buildup from machinery, pipework and engine room surfaces without damaging metal or painted surfaces. They include solvent-based degreasers, alkaline cleaners, descaling acids and cooling system flush chemicals used during periodic maintenance routines. Al-Amin Traders supplies engine room cleaning chemical packages from internationally recognized marine chemical manufacturers for the Bangladesh Navy's maintenance program.

Enquiry

2.5 Ship Safety & Survival Equipment

Life Jackets and Personal Flotation Devices

Life jackets provide buoyancy to keep an unconscious or incapacitated person face-up in the water and are mandatory safety equipment for all persons aboard naval vessels. Naval life jackets are manufactured to SOLAS standards with automatic or manual inflation, retroreflective tape, a light and a whistle for survivor location. Al-Amin Traders supplies SOLAS-approved inflatable and foam-based life jackets in adult sizes along with inspection and service kits for in-date maintenance.

Enquiry

Life Rafts and Inflatable Equipment

Life rafts provide a protected survival platform for vessel crew following abandonment of the ship and are stowed in hydrostatic release cradles for automatic deployment if the vessel sinks. Inflatable equipment also includes rescue boats, man-overboard modules and inflatable rescue platforms deployed in emergency situations. Al-Amin Traders supplies SOLAS-approved life rafts from internationally certified manufacturers and arranges the annual servicing and recertification required to maintain their validity.

Enquiry

Fire Suppression Equipment for Vessels

Naval vessel fire suppression systems protect the engine room, fuel tanks, ammunition magazines and accommodation spaces from fire using CO2, Halon replacement, water mist or foam-based suppression agents. Equipment includes fixed suppression system cylinders, distribution headers, nozzle arrays, actuation systems and associated detection components. Al-Amin Traders supplies fire suppression system components, cylinder refills and complete system installations for Bangladesh Navy vessel fire protection requirements.

Enquiry

Marine Fire Hoses and Nozzles

Marine fire hoses are required at every fire hydrant position throughout a naval vessel and must be maintained in serviceable condition for immediate deployment in a fire emergency. Fire hose nozzles deliver the water stream in a jet or spray pattern as required and include adjustable combination nozzles used on naval vessels. Al-Amin Traders supplies fire hoses in standard naval lengths with aluminium couplings and combination nozzles meeting SOLAS and national naval safety requirements.

Enquiry

Breathing Apparatus for Confined Spaces

Self-contained breathing apparatus (SCBA) allows crew members to enter smoke-filled or oxygen-deficient spaces including engine rooms, cargo holds and damage control compartments during fire or flooding emergencies. Breathing apparatus equipment includes compressed air cylinders, face masks, regulators and harness assemblies maintained in ready-use lockers throughout the vessel. Al-Amin Traders supplies SCBA sets, cylinder hydrostatic testing and refill services and spare components for the breathing apparatus inventory of Bangladesh Navy vessels.

Enquiry

Emergency Signaling Equipment

Emergency signaling equipment allows survival craft and personnel in the water to signal their position to rescue aircraft and vessels using visual and electronic means. Equipment includes SOLAS pyrotechnic flares, EPIRB beacons, SART transponders, signal mirrors and hand-held VHF radios carried in life raft and survival kit packs. Al-Amin Traders supplies pyrotechnic signaling equipment and electronic emergency beacons within their validity periods for Bangladesh Navy vessel safety inventories.

Enquiry

First Aid Kits — Maritime

Maritime first aid kits are stocked to a standard appropriate to the size and voyage duration of the vessel and allow the ship's medical staff to manage trauma, illness and injury until the patient can reach a shore medical facility. Naval vessel medical kits contain a full range of wound management, airway management, diagnostic and medication supplies. Al-Amin Traders supplies maritime medical kits, individual life raft survival packs and replacement medical consumables for the Bangladesh Navy's vessel medical inventories.

Enquiry

2.6 Naval Workshop Supplies

Precision Measuring Instruments

Precision measuring instruments are used in naval workshops to verify the dimensions of machined parts, worn components and replacement items before they are fitted to vessel machinery. Instruments include micrometers, vernier calipers, dial gauges, bore gauges and surface plates calibrated to traceable standards. Al-Amin Traders supplies precision measuring instruments from internationally recognized metrology manufacturers with calibration certificates for the NSSD workshop inventory.

Enquiry

Cutting Tools and Machine Tool Accessories

Naval workshops use lathes, milling machines and drilling machines to manufacture replacement parts and modify components that are no longer available through the supply chain. Cutting tools including turning inserts, milling cutters, drill bits and taps are consumable items replaced at regular intervals. Al-Amin Traders supplies high-speed steel and carbide cutting tools, toolholders and machine tool accessories for the NSSD's fabrication and repair workshop.

Enquiry

Welding Equipment and Consumables

Welding is a core repair process in naval workshops used for hull plating repairs, pipe fabrication, structural modifications and machinery mounting. Welding equipment includes MIG, TIG and arc welding machines along with safety equipment, welding screens and positioners. Consumables include welding rods, wire spools, shielding gas cylinders and flux needed for continuous workshop operation. Al-Amin Traders supplies welding machines and a full range of consumables to the NSSD workshop.

Enquiry

Metal Working Tools and Equipment

General metalworking tools used in naval workshops include bench vises, angle grinders, hacksaws, files, chisels, hammers and power tools needed for fabrication and repair tasks that do not require machine tools. Metal working equipment also covers hydraulic presses, pipe benders, plate rollers and thread-cutting machines used for more complex fabrication work. Al-Amin Traders supplies a comprehensive range of hand and power metalworking tools for the NSSD's workshop and vessel maintenance teams.

Enquiry

Abrasives and Finishing Materials

Abrasives are used throughout naval maintenance for surface preparation before painting, deburring of machined parts, polishing of bearing surfaces and removal of corrosion from steel structures. Finishing materials include grinding discs, flap wheels, sandpaper, wire brushes, polishing compounds and grit blast media used in surface preparation operations. Al-Amin Traders supplies abrasive consumables in the grit sizes and materials required across the NSSD's painting, machining and surface treatment operations.

Enquiry

Workshop Chemicals and Lubricants

Naval workshop chemicals support day-to-day maintenance tasks including metal cleaning, thread lubrication, corrosion prevention, gasket removal and hydraulic testing. Workshop lubricants include cutting oils, tapping fluids, anti-seize compounds and penetrating oils used to free corroded fasteners and lubricate tools during fabrication. Al-Amin Traders supplies workshop chemical packages including degreasers, lubricants, thread compounds and corrosion preventives for the NSSD's maintenance operations.

Enquiry

NDT Equipment — Non-Destructive Testing

Non-destructive testing allows naval engineers to inspect welds, hull plating, shafts and structural components for cracks, voids and thickness loss without dismantling or damaging the component being inspected. NDT methods used in naval workshops include ultrasonic thickness measurement, magnetic particle inspection, dye penetrant testing and radiographic examination. Al-Amin Traders supplies NDT equipment and consumables from internationally recognized manufacturers for the NSSD's quality assurance and structural inspection requirements.

Enquiry

2.7 Vessel Maintenance Materials

Marine Timber and Caulking Materials

Marine timber is used for deck planking, interior joinery, hatch covers and blocking in drydock maintenance of naval vessels. Caulking materials including oakum, seam compound and polyurethane deck caulk are used to seal the gaps between deck planks and prevent water penetration. Al-Amin Traders supplies marine-grade teak and hardwood timbers along with the caulking materials required for deck and joinery maintenance on Bangladesh Navy vessels.

Enquiry

Fiberglass Repair Materials

Fiberglass composite materials are used in the construction of small patrol craft, rigid inflatable boats and superstructure components and require specialized repair materials when damaged. Fiberglass repair materials include woven rovings, chopped strand mat, epoxy and polyester resins, hardeners and release agents used in wet lay-up and vacuum infusion repair techniques. Al-Amin Traders supplies fiberglass repair material kits for the composite vessel and small craft maintenance requirements of the Bangladesh Navy.

Enquiry

Underwater Epoxy and Patching Compounds

Underwater epoxy compounds can be applied to the hull of a vessel below the waterline without drydocking to repair minor corrosion damage, seal small cracks and restore protective coatings. These two-part epoxy systems are formulated to cure in the presence of water and adhere to steel and painted surfaces underwater. Al-Amin Traders supplies underwater repair epoxy and hull patching compounds suitable for emergency and routine underwater hull maintenance by naval dive teams.

Enquiry

Sacrificial Anodes — Zinc, Aluminum and Magnesium

Sacrificial anodes protect the steel hull, propeller shafts, rudders and sea chests of naval vessels from electrolytic corrosion by providing a more active metal that corrodes preferentially. Zinc anodes are used in seawater, aluminum anodes in brackish water and magnesium anodes in fresh water applications. Al-Amin Traders supplies hull anodes in the standard plate, slimline and bolt-on configurations for the drydock maintenance requirements of Bangladesh Navy vessels.

Enquiry

Rubber Marine Fenders and Bumpers

Marine fenders protect the hull of a naval vessel from impact damage when berthing alongside jetties and other vessels. Rubber fenders are manufactured in cylindrical, D-section, W-section and panel configurations and are fixed to the hull or hanging from the vessel's side on ropes. Al-Amin Traders supplies rubber marine fenders in the sizes and section profiles appropriate for the patrol craft and larger vessels of the Bangladesh Navy.

Enquiry

Bilge Pumps and Accessories

Bilge pumps remove accumulated water from the lowest point of the vessel's hull and are a critical piece of safety equipment in damage control and routine maintenance operations. Accessories include bilge pump strainers, automatic float switches, non-return valves, hose connections and control panels used in both fixed installation and portable bilge pumping systems. Al-Amin Traders supplies electric and manual bilge pumps along with all associated accessories and spare parts for Bangladesh Navy vessel bilge management systems.
