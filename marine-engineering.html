2.1 Marine Engineering Components

Marine Diesel Engine Spare Parts

Naval vessels rely on marine diesel engines for main propulsion and auxiliary power and require a constant supply of spare parts to maintain operational availability through scheduled and unscheduled maintenance. Engine spare parts include pistons, liner kits, fuel injection equipment, turbocharger assemblies, cooling system parts and complete overhaul gasket sets matched to the specific engine model in service. Al-Amin Traders sources genuine and OEM-equivalent spare parts for the diesel engine types operated by the Bangladesh Navy.

Marine Gear and Propulsion Components

Marine gearboxes and reduction gears transmit engine power to the propeller shaft at the correct rotational speed and direction for efficient propulsion. Propulsion components include gearbox internals, clutch packs, output flanges, coupling elements and thrust bearings that must withstand the continuous load of open-sea operation. Al-Amin Traders supplies gearbox spare parts and propulsion drive components for naval vessels maintained through the NSSD.

Enquiry

Stern Tube and Propeller Shaft Materials

The stern tube is the watertight housing through which the propeller shaft passes from the engine room through the hull and into the water. Stern tube components include cutlass bearings, lip seals, seal housing assemblies and lubrication systems that prevent water ingress and support the rotating shaft. Al-Amin Traders supplies stern tube and shaft sealing components along with bronze propeller shaft materials for routine and drydock maintenance cycles.

Enquiry

Marine Pumps and Pump Components

Marine pumps are used throughout a naval vessel for sea water cooling, fresh water distribution, bilge drainage, fuel transfer, firefighting and ballast management. Pump components include impellers, wear rings, mechanical seals, shaft sleeves and bearing assemblies that degrade over time and require regular replacement. Al-Amin Traders supplies centrifugal, gear and diaphragm pump assemblies and all associated wearing parts for the pump types in service with the Bangladesh Navy.

Enquiry

Marine Compressors and Accessories

Marine compressors supply compressed air for engine starting, torpedo systems, diving operations, control systems and general shipboard pneumatic tools and equipment. Accessories include air receivers, pressure relief valves, moisture separators, after-coolers and compressor valve assemblies used in maintenance and overhaul. Al-Amin Traders supplies high-pressure and low-pressure marine compressor assemblies and the consumable parts required for scheduled overhaul intervals.

Enquiry

Heat Exchangers and Cooling System Parts

Marine heat exchangers transfer heat from engine jacket water, lubricating oil and charge air to sea water cooling circuits, maintaining engine temperatures within safe operating limits. Cooling system parts include heat exchanger bundles, end covers, sacrificial anodes, expansion tanks, thermostats and coolant hoses. Al-Amin Traders supplies heat exchanger units and cooling system components for the diesel engine and auxiliary machinery plants of Bangladesh Navy vessels.

Enquiry

Marine Gaskets, Seals and O-Rings

Marine gaskets and seals are critical consumable items that prevent leakage of oil, water, fuel and gas at every joint and connection point throughout the machinery plant of a naval vessel. O-rings, flange gaskets, cylinder head gaskets, valve stem seals and stuffing box packing are replaced at every maintenance interval to maintain system integrity. Al-Amin Traders supplies comprehensive gasket and seal kits in the materials and dimensions required for all engine and mechanical system overhauls.
