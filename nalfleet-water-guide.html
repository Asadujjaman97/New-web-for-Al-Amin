Frequently Asked Questions About Boiler Chemicals

What chemicals are used in Nalfleet boiler water treatment

Nalfleet boiler water treatment uses advanced scale inhibitors corrosion inhibitors oxygen scavengers alkalinity builders and sludge conditioners to maintain safe boiler operation improve heat transfer efficiency and ensure long term system protection.

Why is oxygen scavenger required in Nalfleet boiler programs

Nalfleet oxygen scavengers remove dissolved oxygen from boiler feed water preventing oxygen corrosion pitting and premature boiler tube failure while protecting metal surfaces.

How does boiler scale affect boiler efficiency in Nalco systems

Boiler scale acts as an insulating layer on heat transfer surfaces increasing fuel consumption reducing efficiency and causing localized overheating of boiler tubes. Nalco treatment programs prevent scale formation and maintain optimal performance.

Which industries use Nalco boiler water treatment in Bangladesh
