ASR OPTITREAT 7359
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Treatment Chemical

High performance liquid cooling tower treatment combining scale control corrosion inhibition and pH stabilization for efficient cooling tower operation. Ensures clean system surfaces improved heat transfer and long term protection.

ASR OPTITREAT 449
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Treatment Chemical

Advanced cooling tower treatment for corrosion and scale control. It protects metal surfaces reduces deposits improves heat transfer and enhances system efficiency. Ideal for industrial cooling towers and water circulation systems for long term reliable performance.

ASR OPTITREAT 149
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Treatment Chemical

Advanced cooling tower treatment chemical that controls scale and corrosion in open and closed systems. It protects equipment improves heat transfer and ensures efficient long term system performance.

(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower pH Controller

Effective pH controller for cooling tower water that maintains balanced pH prevents corrosion and scale formation and improves system efficiency. Suitable for industrial cooling systems it ensures stable operation protects equipment and supports long term performance.

ASR OPTITREAT DT 288
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower pH Controller

Effective pH controller for cooling tower water that maintains balanced pH prevents corrosion and scale formation and enhances system efficiency. Ideal for industrial cooling systems it supports stable operation protects equipment and ensures long term performance.

ASR BIOCIDE ST 40
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Biocide

Powerful oxidizing biocide for cooling tower systems that controls bacteria algae and slime. It keeps water clean prevents biological growth reduces fouling and ensures efficient and stable system operation. Ideal for industrial cooling towers to maintain hygiene and long term performance.

BIOCIDE ST 70
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Biocide

High-strength oxidizing biocide for rapid microbial control in cooling towers. Ideal for periodic shock dosing in heavily contaminated systems it eliminates bacteria algae and slime, prevents fouling and maintains clean efficient operation.

ASR BIOCIDE OX
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Biocide

Versatile oxidizing biocide for cooling tower systems that controls microbial growth and biofilm formation. It ensures clean water reduces fouling improves system hygiene and maintains reliable long term operation.

ASR BIOCIDE 7330
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Biocide

Concentrated non oxidizing biocide for long term microbial control in cooling systems. It suppresses bacteria algae and biofilm, is compatible with different water chemistries, and ensures clean efficient and reliable operation.

ASR BIOCIDE F 501
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Biocide

Non oxidizing biocide for broad spectrum control of bacteria and fungi in cooling systems. It is safe for sensitive systems provides effective microbial protection and ensures clean stable and efficient operation.

ASR BIOCIDE 90001
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Biocide

Premium non oxidizing biocide for sustained microbial control in cooling systems. It provides long lasting protection without affecting metallurgy and is suitable for continuous dosing to maintain clean and efficient operation.

ASR BIODISPERSANT 901
(Liquid Form)
Cooling Tower Biodispersant

Cooling tower biodispersant for open recirculating systems that removes biofilm and deposits. It improves biocide efficiency enhances water circulation and ensures clean and stable system performance.
