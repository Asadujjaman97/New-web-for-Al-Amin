Ordnance Supplies

Al-Amin Traders is a DGDP enlisted supplier serving BOF, COD, Bangladesh Air Force and Bangladesh Army with ammunition and ordnance support materials, and explosives and demolition support materials. For general military supplies (vehicle parts, field equipment, clothing, electronics and workshop supplies), see our Military Supplies page.

1.1 Ammunition & Ordnance Support Materials

Propellant Chemicals and Precursor Raw Materials

Propellant chemicals are the energetic compounds used in the manufacture of ammunition, driving the projectile through the barrel upon ignition. Precursor raw materials include the base chemical inputs required in controlled production environments under strict regulatory compliance. Al-Amin Traders sources these from internationally approved manufacturers and supplies them through Bangladesh's formal defence procurement channels.

Enquiry

Primer and Ignition Components

Primers are the small explosive devices seated at the base of a cartridge that ignite the propellant charge when struck by the firing pin. Ignition components cover the full range of initiator elements used across pistol, rifle and artillery ammunition. These are precision-manufactured items requiring strict quality certification and are supplied to meet BOF production and maintenance requirements.

Enquiry

Cartridge Case Materials — Brass and Steel Alloys

Cartridge cases form the outer shell that houses the primer, propellant and projectile in a complete round of ammunition. Brass is the most widely used material due to its corrosion resistance and malleability while steel alloys are used in higher-volume or cost-optimized production. Al-Amin Traders supplies both raw materials and formed cartridge case components to ordnance manufacturing facilities.

Enquiry

Explosive Initiator Components

Explosive initiator components are the devices that begin the detonation sequence in ordnance systems, converting a mechanical, electrical or chemical signal into an explosive output. They include detonators, igniters, delay elements and bridgewire assemblies used across a wide range of munitions. Supply of these items requires full compliance with Bangladesh Ministry of Defence import and storage regulations.

Enquiry

Packaging Materials for Ordnance — Specialized Crates, Foam Inserts and Moisture-Proof Liners

Ordnance packaging must protect ammunition and explosive components against shock, vibration, moisture and temperature change during transport and long-term storage. Specialized wooden and metal crates, precision-cut foam inserts and moisture-proof barrier liners are all required to meet military specification standards. Al-Amin Traders supplies packaging materials that conform to STANAG and equivalent defence packaging standards.

Enquiry

Ordnance Inspection and Testing Equipment

Inspection and testing equipment is used at ordnance facilities to verify the dimensional accuracy, functional integrity and safety of manufactured or received ammunition batches. Equipment includes gauge sets, visual inspection systems, pressure testing devices and electronic function testers. Al-Amin Traders sources these instruments from international suppliers and ensures calibration certification is included with each delivery.

Enquiry

Ballistic Testing Instruments

Ballistic testing instruments measure the performance parameters of ammunition including muzzle velocity, pressure levels, projectile stability and terminal ballistic behavior. These are used by ordnance quality control teams to validate production batches against specification before acceptance into the defence inventory. Al-Amin Traders supplies chronographs, pressure measurement systems and ballistic data recording equipment from certified international sources.

1.7 Explosives & Demolition Support Materials

Demolition Equipment — Non-Explosive Components

Demolition operations require a range of non-explosive support equipment including crimping tools, firing wire reels, blasting machine testers, circuit testers and demolition kit cases used by combat engineer units. These items form the infrastructure around which explosive charges are connected, tested and fired in controlled demolition tasks. Al-Amin Traders supplies non-explosive demolition tools and kits from international manufacturers to support engineer unit procurement requirements.

Enquiry

Blasting Accessories

Blasting accessories are the non-explosive items that connect, initiate and control explosive charges in demolition, mining and construction operations conducted by military engineer units. They include blasting caps, electric detonators, detonating cord connectors, delay elements and lead wire assemblies. Al-Amin Traders supplies blasting accessories from internationally licensed manufacturers through the appropriate regulatory and DGDP procurement channels.

Enquiry

Safety Fuse and Detonating Cord Materials

Safety fuse provides a controlled burning rate that allows the operator to move to a safe distance before igniting a detonator. Detonating cord transmits a detonating wave at high velocity and is used to connect multiple charges in a simultaneous or sequenced firing network. Al-Amin Traders supplies both types in standard coil and reel lengths certified to the burn rate and velocity specifications required by military engineer units.

Enquiry

Mine Detection Equipment Accessories

Mine detection accessories support the operation and maintenance of mine detector systems used by engineer units in route clearance and minefield breaching tasks. Accessories include search coil assemblies, control unit batteries, headsets, probe rods and calibration test pieces required for daily equipment checks and field repairs. Al-Amin Traders supplies spare parts and accessories for in-service mine detection systems operated by the Bangladesh Armed Forces.

Enquiry

EOD Tools and Kits — Explosive Ordnance Disposal

EOD tools and kits are used by trained disposal technicians to safely approach, render safe and remove or destroy unexploded ordnance and improvised explosive devices. A standard EOD kit contains hook and line sets, disruptors, render-safe tools, protective equipment, x-ray systems and remote handling aids. Al-Amin Traders supplies EOD tool kits and individual replacement components from internationally qualified manufacturers to Bangladesh's explosive ordnance disposal units.
